K-5 Professional Development

  • Code.org K-5 Modules: Consider 'Computer Science Basics for K-5 Teachers', 'Teaching Computer Science Fundamentals', and 'Teaching Computer Science Connections'
  • Find Facilitator-Led Workshops with Code.org
  • CSTA Professional Learning Series Lectures (Note: Requires CSTA+ Membership)
  • CSTA Online Courses (Note: between $39-$99, or free with CSTA+ Membership)

6-12 Professional Development

  • Code.org Middle SchoolModules: Consider 'Teaching Computer Science Discoveries', 'Teaching Problem Solving and Computing', and any pertinent sub-content area courses(Circuits, WebDev, etc)
  • Code.org High School Modules Consider 'Teaching Computer Science Principles' and any pertinent sub-content area courses(AI, Data and Society, etc)
  • CodeHS PD Opportunities for 9-12
  • CSTA Professional Learning Series Lectures (Note: Requires CSTA+ Membership)
  • CSTA Online Courses (Note: between $39-$99, or free with CSTA+ Membership)
